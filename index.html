Our Lab
Our lab focuses on high-level cognition: How are concepts represented in the brain, and what are the mechanisms of semantic integration? What is the function of conscious awareness, and which brain processes are involved? More specifically, the lab explores the cognitive and neural underpinnings of semantically judging the relations between objects or concepts, and studies the role of consciousness in integrating semantic information.
